Tools

We provide you with 4 tools to detect fake news and stop it from spreading.

chrome extention

Our Chrome Extension detects fake news articles while you browse social media.

web app

Our web app provides an easy way to paste the URL for a news story to see if it’s real or fake.

mobile app

Our mobile app does everything the web app can, but conveniently on your phone.

hunter's portal

If you're a journalist or fact checker, this is for you. The Hunters Portal gives you a direct way to make sure your readers have a reliable source of information when reading the news on social media and around the web.

Chrome Extention

Identifies fake news sources while you browse.

magic highlighting

When NewsHound detects fake, questionable or factually untrue news stories, it highlights it directly in your news feed.

debug misleading content

The extension helps to debunk malicious or misleading content and stops it from spreading virally by providing facts and rebuttal articles.

web app

Copy and paste a URL from a news article into the NewsHound web app and it will tell you whether the content is unreliable or trustworthy.

simplicity

The web app uses a simple search bar that’s connected to the NewsHound database to determine where a news article is coming from and whether you can trust it or not.

speed

The web app is the fastest way to get more information about the sources of news articles shared across all kind of social platforms.
